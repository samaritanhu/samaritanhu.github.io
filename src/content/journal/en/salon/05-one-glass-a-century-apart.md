🚶 Reading at City Lights in North Beach, San Francisco. 📚

This was once a gathering place for the Beat writers—Jack Kerouac, for one. Reading and drinking here, I feel as though I am breathing the same air as someone from a century ago, drinking from the same glass. Two eras briefly make eye contact.
